What is meant by mental health?.


According to the World Health Organization (WHO): “Mental health is a state of well-being in which an individual realizes his or her own abilities, can cope with the normal stresses of life, can work productively, and is able to make a contribution to his or her community.

Now the most important part of all of this is that we humans don't realize that everybody goes through problems and that sometimes it is okay, to not be okay. Following are some people who came from nothing,faced problems and now are an inspiration for the world.


Michael Crossland

The guy in the above video is Michael Crossland. He had his mom as his supporter and motivator. In simple terms his story illustrates that if you have the right people around, you can fight through anything. In life we have different types of people surrounding us. Some pretend to be our friends while some are actually there for us when we need them. Its better to identify fake people and get rid of them.


Mo Samuels

This is Mo Samuels. Mo was diagnosed with lymphoedema at the age of 12, which causes swelling in his body's tissues, which meant that he was more susceptible to illness. Then in 2013, he developed an infection in his knee, causing him to wake up in the middle of the night vomiting and suffering from agonising pain. But he did't give up there and started to train with the goal of being able to walk again. He carried a vision and turned around his life to be a competitive bodybuilder. His story emphasis that having a vision in life about your destination and working hard with consistency leads you towards success. It also teaches that a person should turn his problems into opportunities rather than crying about them.


Rock The Dwayne Johnson

We all know Rock for his electrifying personality and bodybuilding but few of us know that he has reached here after a lot of hardships. In the above video he states that you need to remember the hard times in your life when you are working towards your goals. He also says that you need to have a vision and stay focused on your vision no matter what obstacles get in your way to reach your ultimate destination.
